# Career Development & Guidance

Navigate your technology career path with personalized guidance. This command helps you plan career transitions, understand market opportunities, and develop long-term professional growth strategies.

## Usage

Use this command to:
- **Plan Transitions**: Move between roles or specializations
- **Career Strategy**: Develop long-term career goals
- **Market Insights**: Understand job market and opportunities
- **Salary Negotiation**: Know your market value
- **Leadership Growth**: Transition to management
- **Specialization Paths**: Develop deep expertise
- **Work-Life Balance**: Sustainable career planning

## Career Paths in Technology

### Individual Contributor (IC) Track
Professional growth while staying hands-on with technology.

**Career Progression:**
- Junior Developer → Developer → Senior Developer → Staff/Principal Engineer

**Characteristics:**
- Deep technical expertise
- Problem-solving focus
- Code quality emphasis
- Technical leadership
- Architecture influence
- Mentoring others

**When to pursue:**
- You love technical challenges
- Prefer autonomy over management
- Want to be a technical authority
- Enjoy staying hands-on

### Management Track
Leadership-focused career building teams and products.

**Career Progression:**
- Developer → Tech Lead → Engineering Manager → Senior Manager → Director

**Characteristics:**
- People leadership
- Team building and growth
- Strategic planning
- Project delivery
- Stakeholder management
- Organizational impact

**When to pursue:**
- You enjoy mentoring people
- Prefer strategic planning
- Like building teams
- Want broader organizational impact

### Hybrid Track
Balanced approach combining technical expertise with leadership.

**Career Progression:**
- Developer → Senior Developer → Tech Lead/Architect → Engineering Manager

**Characteristics:**
- Technical credibility
- Leadership skills
- Strategic thinking
- Hands-on when needed
- Team oversight
- Architecture influence

**Flexibility:** Move between IC and management tracks

### Specialized Expert Track
Becoming world-class in specific domain.

**Specializations Available:**
- **Security Expert**: Cybersecurity, penetration testing, compliance
- **Performance Engineer**: System optimization, profiling, scaling
- **Architecture Specialist**: System design, enterprise architecture
- **DevOps Expert**: Infrastructure, deployment, reliability
- **Data Scientist**: ML models, analytics, AI systems
- **AI/ML Specialist**: Deep learning, LLMs, AI applications
- **Cloud Architect**: Cloud platform expertise

## Transitioning Between Roles

### Frontend → Backend Transition
**Timeline:** 3-6 months (with existing programming skills)
1. Learn backend language/framework (2-3 months)
2. Build backend projects (1-2 months)
3. Contribute to backend systems (1 month)
4. Formal role transition

### Developer → Tech Lead
**Timeline:** 1-2 years
1. Excel as senior developer (6-12 months)
2. Mentor junior developers (6 months)
3. Lead technical decisions (6 months)
4. Formal tech lead role

### IC → Management Transition
**Timeline:** 2-3 years
1. Become strong senior developer
2. Mentor and support others
3. Help with hiring/onboarding
4. Shadow managers, learn processes
5. Manager training/coaching
6. First management role

### Full Stack → Specialization
**Timeline:** 6-12 months
1. Deepen knowledge in chosen area
2. Build specialized projects
3. Lead specialist initiatives
4. Become go-to expert
5. Specialist role or title change

## Salary & Compensation

Factors affecting compensation:
1. **Experience Level**: Junior < Senior < Staff < Principal
2. **Location**: Silicon Valley > NYC > Austin > Midwest
3. **Company Size**: FAANG > Startups > Small companies
4. **Role Demand**: High-demand roles command premiums
5. **Specialization**: Rare skills worth more
6. **Negotiation**: Proper negotiation critical

### Salary Negotiation Tips
- **Research**: Know market rates for your role/location
- **Timing**: Negotiate before accepting, during raises
- **Documentation**: Track accomplishments and impact
- **Multiple Offers**: Leverage for better terms
- **Total Comp**: Consider equity, benefits, flexibility
- **Growth**: Negotiate growth opportunities
