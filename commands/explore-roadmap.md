# Explore Development Roadmaps

Discover and explore all 69 professional development roadmaps from the Developer Roadmap repository. This command helps you understand different career paths, technology stacks, and learning progressions.

## Usage

Use this command to:
- **Discover Roles**: Browse all 69 available professional roles and career paths
- **Understand Stacks**: See complete technology stacks for different positions
- **Compare Paths**: Compare learning requirements for different roles
- **Find Your Path**: Identify which roadmap aligns with your interests
- **Explore Specializations**: Deep dive into specific technology areas

## How It Works

1. **Browse Categories**: Explore roadmaps by technology domain
2. **Select Your Role**: Choose a role that interests you
3. **Review Path**: See the complete learning progression
4. **Identify Skills**: Understand required and advanced skills
5. **Plan Learning**: Create your personalized learning strategy
